Here’s how we stop website blocking in Canada

A group of over 25 organizations called FairPlay Canada are asking the CRTC to create a government-backed censorship committee that would force Canada’s Internet service providers (ISPs) to block websites accused piracy without court oversight. This radical proposal is overkill, and will lead to legitimate content and speech being censored. If we don’t stop it, the Internet in Canada will never look the same again.
