Here’s how we stop website blocking in Canada

A coalition of over 25 organizations called FairPlay Canada is asking the CRTC to create a government-backed censorship committee that would force Canada’s Internet service providers (ISPs) to block websites accused of piracy without court oversight. This SOPA-style proposal will lead to legitimate content and speech being censored. Here’s how internet users can help stop it:
